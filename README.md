## Cold-Call Angles

### 1. "You respond in 5 minutes in person — does your website respond that fast?"
Their whole pitch is speed. But if someone fills out a contact form at 9pm and doesn't hear back until morning, that's not 5 minutes — it's 12 hours. An automated confirmation text fires instantly and keeps them in the game overnight.

### 2. "Your 24/7 emergency line is your best feature — is it on your site?"
Emergency HVAC calls are the highest-urgency, highest-conversion leads. The demo has a click-to-call emergency button that's impossible to miss on mobile. Ask: "If someone's AC dies at 11pm and they find your site, can they reach you in one tap?"

### 3. Maintenance plan capture = recurring revenue
The demo includes a maintenance plan section. If Southern Solutions offers any kind of annual plan, a website that sells it passively is pure recurring revenue with no extra sales effort.

### 4. "You're competing with big franchises — this is how you beat them"
Companies like One Hour HVAC have huge budgets but terrible local reputation. Southern Solutions wins on trust and speed — the site makes that obvious from the first click.
